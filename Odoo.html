Odoo ERP System: What is it?

One of the most comprehensive business management programs on the market today is Odoo, which can handle every aspect of a company’s operations via its assigned modules.

Fabien Pinckaers founded the company in 2005, and their first software product, TinyERP, was later renamed as Odoo. As the platform grew rapidly, it was renamed Odoo starting with Version 8. The programmers released two platform versions starting with version 9 of the program as it proceeded with its business management features.

There are differences in various operating capabilities between the business and community editions. The most recent version of the Odoo, V16, is said to be among the quickest and most sophisticated versions ever developed.

What Distinguishes Odoo ERP from its rivals?

Open-source Odoo ERP is regarded as one of the most sophisticated and dependable corporate operations management technologies available. The Odoo platform is unique in the following ways, which are highlighted here:

  • Wide varity of company management features
  • Proven ERP with over 7 million users
  • No bendor lock in the software
  • 40,560+ compatible add-ons accessible via the Odoo applications store
  • A committed Odoo community to help users of platform.

Jus why choose Odoo ERP

Why not, is the response. The Odoo ERP’s application-specific modules are integrated into a dedicated modular business operations framework to provide dependable, economical, and efficient business operations and management. In addition, the Odoo ERP platform has a consolidated approach to operations, using a single central inventory management system to efficiently handle the company’s retail, wholesale, and e-commerce activities. Furthermore, all of the company’s information is kept in a single central database, facilitating efficient communication and information sharing inside and outside of the business.
